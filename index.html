Request Account and Data Deletion

To request that your account and all associated data be deleted from XrAId, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at xraid2025@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."
  2. Include your registered email address and any other details necessary to identify your account.
  3. We will process your request within 7-10 business days.

Once your account is deleted, all your data will be permanently removed.
